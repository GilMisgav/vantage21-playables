## 📸 SHOT LIST

1. **Hook close-up** — Phone arm's-length, creator leaning IN fast, slightly off-center, natural light.
2. **Reaction wide-ish** — Pull back so hand + screen are visible, shows it's "live."
3. **Over-the-shoulder peek** — Camera catches actual gameplay screen for legitimacy.
4. **Balance zoom** — Quick punch-in / phone tilt toward camera on the number going up.
5. **Genuine laugh moment** — Looking away then back, "I can't believe it" energy.
6. **CTA eye-contact** — Final shot dead-center, talking straight to viewer.

**Creator notes:** Keep it shaky-handheld, no studio polish. One imperfect take > perfect. Real ambient room sound under VO.

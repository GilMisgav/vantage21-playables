🎬 UGC creatives

Animated video concepts (animatics) + their scripts. Previews autoplay — use them as motion creatives, or as the animatic to brief a real creator shoot.

To add a variation: save your HTML in variations/, then add an entry to the VARIATIONS array at the top of this file's script. Fields: title, file, desc, tags, status (live/draft), game, updated. See README.md for the full guide.
